Description

This flipchart covers a week of lessons for 3D shapes in a Year 1 classroom. Features of these lessons include:

  • Flat surface
  • Curved surface
  • Connecting 3D shapes in the real world
  • Faces of a 3D shape
  • Types of prisms
  • Types of faces
  • Edges
  • Surfaces and features
  • 3D shapes in common everyday objects

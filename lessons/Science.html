Science

by Laurel Newton


Description

This flipchart covers a term lessons for the School Yard Safari unit in a Year 1 classroom. Features of these lessons include:

  • Predicting animals that may be found in the school yard
  • Role play activity
  • Code for caring
  • Facts about Earthworms
  • Facts about Snails
  • Facts about Ants
  • Design a habitat
  • Create a habitat
